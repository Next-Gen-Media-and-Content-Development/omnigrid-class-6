Science ek systematic aur organized knowledge hai jo observation aur experiments ke zariye natural world ko samajhne ki koshish karti hai.

Word "Science" Latin word "Scientia" se aaya hai — jiska matlab hai "Knowledge" yaani Gyaan.

Science ki 3 main branches hain:

  • 🔵 Physics — Force, energy, motion aur matter ka study
  • 🟢 Chemistry — Matter ki composition aur chemical reactions
  • 🟡 Biology — Living organisms ka study

💡 Key Insight

Science mein curiosity sabse zaroori hai! Har badi discovery ek simple question se shuru hui — "Kyun?" ya "Kaise?"
💡 FUN FACT
The word "Hypothesis" comes from Greek — hypo (under) + thesis (placing). It literally means "putting something forward to be tested"! Scientists have been doing this for over 2,000 years. 🌞

Scientific Method

Scientific Method ek systematic process hai jisse scientists problems solve karte hain aur naye discoveries karte hain.

🔄 Observation → Question → Hypothesis → Experiment → Data → Conclusion

Har step ka matlab:

  • 👀 Observation: Apni senses use karke kuch notice karo
  • ❓ Question: "Kyun?" ya "Kaise?" poochho
  • 💡 Hypothesis: Testable prediction banao
  • ⚗️ Experiment: Controlled test karo
  • 📊 Data Collection: Results record karo
  • ✅ Conclusion: Analyze karo — hypothesis sahi thi ya nahi?

⚠️ Important!

Agar hypothesis wrong prove ho — yeh bhi valuable result hai! Science mein failure nahi, sirf learning hoti hai 🌱
🔍 EXTRA FACT
Galileo Galilei (1564–1642) was one of the first scientists to use the Scientific Method systematically. He proved that objects of different weights fall at the same speed — by dropping balls from the Leaning Tower of Pisa! 🏔

Laboratory Safety Rules

Laboratory mein kaam karte waqt safety sabse pehli priority hai! Chemicals aur equipment dangerous ho sakte hain agar carefully handle na kiya jaye.

🛡️ Must Follow Rules:

  • 👓 Hamesha safety glasses aur apron pehno
  • 🚫 Chemicals ko kabhi bhi taste mat karo
  • 👩‍🏫 Experiments sirf teacher ki supervision mein karo
  • 🧼 Kaam ke baad haath acche se dhoo lo
  • 🍎 Lab mein khaana khana mana hai
  • 🚨 Koi bhi accident pe turant teacher ko batao
  • 🔥 Flame ke paas loose kapde mat pehno

Famous Scientists

India ne science ko bahut bade discoveries diye hain. Yeh hain kuch scientists jinhe Class 6 mein jaanna zaroori hai:

  • 🏅 C.V. Raman — Raman Effect (1928) • Nobel Prize 1930 mein mila
  • 🚀 APJ Abdul Kalam — Missile technology, India ka space programme
  • ⚛️ Homi Bhabha — India ka nuclear programme
  • 🔢 Aryabhata — Zero (0) ka concept aur Pi ki value
  • 🌟 Srinivasa Ramanujan — Mathematical genius

🌟 Did You Know?

C.V. Raman pehle Asian the jo Physics mein Nobel Prize jeete — 1930 mein!
