🔬 Science kya hai?

Definition

  • Science ek systematic knowledge hai jo observation aur experiments pe based hai
  • Science natural world ko samajhne ki koshish karta hai
  • "Science" word Latin "Scientia" se aaya hai — jiska matlab hai "knowledge"
  • Science ke 3 main branches hain: Physics, Chemistry, Biology

🧪 Scientific Method

Steps of Scientific Method

  • Observation: Kuch interesting dekho ya question aaye
  • Question: "Kyun?" ya "Kaise?" poochho
  • Hypothesis: Ek possible answer predict karo
  • Experiment: Test karo apni hypothesis ko
  • Data Collection: Results record karo
  • Conclusion: Hypothesis sahi thi ya galat?
Observation → Question → Hypothesis → Experiment → Conclusion

⚗️ Laboratory Safety Rules

Important Safety Rules

  • Lab mein hamesha apron aur safety glasses pehno
  • Chemicals ko kabhi bhi taste mat karo
  • Experiments sirf teacher ki supervision mein karo
  • Chemicals ko skin pe lagane se bachao
  • Lab mein khaana mat khao
  • Koi bhi accident hone pe turant teacher ko batao

🌟 Famous Scientists

Indian Scientists

  • C.V. Raman: Raman Effect — light scattering ka discovery
  • APJ Abdul Kalam: Missile technology aur space science
  • Homi Bhabha: India ka nuclear programme
  • Aryabhata: Zero ka concept aur astronomy
